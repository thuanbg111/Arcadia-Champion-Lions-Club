Arcadia Champion Lions Club is a nonprofit organization operating in California, under the model of a Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation. The organization has a tax identification number (EIN): 371980418 — this number is used for donations, issuing tax-deductible receipts, and verifying tax-exempt status with the IRS.

We focus on community service programs such as education, healthcare, emergency relief during natural disasters, environmental protection, and youth development. All activities are carried out in the spirit of “We Serve” with a priority on creating meaningful impact for local residents.

The organization is committed to transparency, compliance with federal and state regulations, and efficient use of resources. Donors and partners can request program reports, tax-deductible receipts, or legal verification based on the Articles of Incorporation and the IRS determination letter.
