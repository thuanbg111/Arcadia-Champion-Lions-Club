Policies

Privacy Policy

We only collect minimal data when you contact or donate: full name, email, contact content and necessary transaction information. The purpose is to respond to requests, manage volunteer/donation activities, and improve services.

Data Sharing: Do not sell data to third parties. Only share with contracted email/payment processing service providers and legal requirements (if any).

Storage time: as required by tax/accounting law or until the purpose of the processing is fulfilled.

Your rights: request access, correction, deletion; object/terminate processing; withdraw consent (if based on consent). Please contact us via the email listed on the Contact page.

Security: apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No method is absolutely secure; we still make every effort to minimize risks.

Children: does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Terms of Use

When using the website, you agree not to post content that violates the law, is harmful, or violates the privacy of others. The content on the website is for informational purposes only and is subject to change; we are not responsible for any damages arising from misuse.

Do not copy/exploit content without written permission, except as permitted by law.

Donations & Tax Deductible Receipts

All donations to Arcadia Champion Lions Club (EIN: 371980418) may be eligible for tax deductions under applicable law. Receipt will be provided via email according to the information you provided.

Refund: If there is a mistake in the transfer or a technical error, please contact us within 7 days for support.

Non-Discrimination & Political Activity

The organization is committed to not discriminating on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, sex, age, disability or any protected status.

As a charity, we do not endorse political candidates and limit lobbying to the extent permitted by law.

Transparency & Reporting

We maintain program/fund records and provide information upon valid request. Legal documents such as Articles of Incorporation and the IRS confirmation letter can be viewed under “Legal Documents”.
